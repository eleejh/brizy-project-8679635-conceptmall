HOW TO MAKE IT

Disclaimer

Servings: 2-3

Preparation: 20m

Cooking: 30m

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 large bell peppers, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/3 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 1/2 pounds flank steak, cut into thin strips

Cooking Steps:

Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook onion, bell peppers, and garlic in oil until tender-crisp, stirring frequently.

Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Pour soy sauce, honey, and red wine vinegar in pan, then add the beef.

Cook beef, stirring frequently, until done, about 10 to 15 minutes. Stir in cooked vegetables, and cook another 10 to 15 m.
